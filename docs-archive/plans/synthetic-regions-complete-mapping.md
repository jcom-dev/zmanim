## Approach: Shared Synthetic Regions

Instead of creating one region per country, we **reuse synthetic regions** where it makes sense:
- Disputed territories share regions based on the administering power
- Overseas territories share regions based on the sovereign nation
- Research stations share one region
- City-states and island nations get their own region (named after the country)

## New Region Type

Add to `geo_region_types` table:
```sql
INSERT INTO geo_region_types (code, name, overture_subtype, sort_order)
VALUES ('locality_group', 'Locality Group', NULL, 100);
```
